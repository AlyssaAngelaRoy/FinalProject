ABOUT SDG 16

UnityConnect: Fighting racism with Unity

Sustainable Development Goal 16 (SDG 16) is one of the 17 goals

established by the United Nations as part of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development.

This SDG focuses on promoting inclusivity, peaceful communities,

providing justice, and building institutions for all.

Their key targets are:

  • Reducing violence
  • Ensuring Equal access to justice
  • Developing effective and accountable institutions
  • Reducing corruption and bribery
