UnityConnect: Fighting racism with Unity

This is a website dedicated in fighting racism by promoting unity, understanding, and equality amongst our peers.

This website aims to raise awareness about systematic racism,

to add educational resources, and to address these racial inequalities.

Which, in turn, aligns with the chosen SDG (SDG 16), which is the goal of promoting peace, justice, and strong institutions.
