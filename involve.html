INVOLVE YOURSELF AGAINST XENOPHOBIA

UnityConnect: Fighting racism with Unity

Now, How do you Combat Xenophobia?

1. Speak out

Challenge racist and xenophobic remarks if you ever encounter them.

2. Participate in awareness campaigns

Include yourself and join or support awareness campaigns against xenophobia.

3. To use your media wisely

Share stories, experiences that are against xenophobia. Amplify the voices of others who speak about it but are not heard.

4. Create inclusive spaces for others

Create an organization, or if you are in one, advocate for practices that include diversity and inclusivity

5. Organize cultural events

Create or join cultural events that celebrate different cultures to promote unity and understanding.
